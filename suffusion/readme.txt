
For Suffusion WordPress Theme users:

Suffusion theme has special support for Now Reading plugin. To update the templates in Suffusion to the latest (if they aren't done already) simply copy the *contents* of this folder into wp-content/themes/suffusion/now-reading/.

You may further customize them as you see fit. For example, you might want to remove the graph and statisitcs by commenting out the print_book_stats() call.
